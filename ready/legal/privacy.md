### What we collect
- **When you enquire or order:** your name, email address, phone number (if given), the type of
  speech, the date of your occasion, and the details of your message.
- **The material for your speech:** the stories, names, memories and information you choose to share
  so we can write your speech. This can include details about you and third parties (e.g. the bride,
  groom or the person being remembered).
- **Reviews:** if you leave a review, this is handled by our reviews provider, reviews.io.
- **Website usage:** basic analytics data (see Cookies below).

### Why we use it, and our lawful basis
- To respond to your enquiry and provide the speech-writing service you've asked for — **performance
  of a contract**, and our **legitimate interest** in answering enquiries.
- To take payment and keep business records — **legal obligation** and contract.
- To send you your draft(s) and revisions — contract.
- To display reviews and improve the service — **consent** / legitimate interest.

We do **not** sell your data, and we do **not** use the personal stories you share for anything
other than writing your speech.

### Who we share it with
We use trusted service providers who process data on our behalf:
- **Hosting & database:** Lovable Cloud / Supabase (stores website and contact-form data).
- **Reviews:** reviews.io.
- **Email:** [your email provider, e.g. Google Workspace].
- **Payments:** [your payment provider, e.g. Stripe / PayPal] — they handle card details; we don't
  store them.
We only share what's necessary, and we never pass your details to third parties for marketing.

### How long we keep it
- Enquiry and project data: for the duration of the project and up to **[e.g. 12 months]** afterwards
  in case you return.
- Financial records: as required by UK law (currently **6 years**).
- We delete the personal stories and material for your speech on request once your project is complete.

### Your rights (UK GDPR)
You have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict or object to our use of your data, and to
data portability. To exercise any of these, email adrian@allspeechesgreatandsmall.com. You also have
the right to complain to the Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).
