# About Adrian Simpson

*The UK's most highly rated and reviewed professional speech writer.*

## The short version
I'm Adrian Simpson, and I write speeches for a living — best man, groom, father of the bride, maid of honour, and eulogies. I've been doing it professionally since 2012, and in that time I've become the most reviewed wedding speech writer in the country. Every speech I write is original, personal, and built entirely around the person giving it. No templates, no recycled jokes, no clichés.

## How I got here
My career began in print. I started out as a magazine journalist in London — a motoring writer at Dennis Publishing — learning the craft that still underpins everything I do: how to find the story, how to make a point land, and how to make people feel something in just a few hundred words.

From there I moved into television, where I spent fifteen years as a presenter, including on the BBC's *Top Gear*. Broadcasting taught me the other half of the job: how words work out loud, how to hold a room, and how timing turns a good line into a great one. Writing for the page and performing for an audience are different skills — and a wedding speech needs both.

In 2012 I brought the two together and founded All Speeches Great & Small. More than a decade later, I've written hundreds of speeches for people who were certain they couldn't do it themselves — and watched them deliver the moment of the day.

## How I work
I don't have a drawer of stock jokes or a template I drop your names into. I can't — because the whole point is that your speech sounds like *you*.

So I listen. We talk about the people, the memories and the moments. I find the story only you can tell, and I write it in your voice — funny where it should be funny, moving where it should be moving, and always true to you. Then we refine it together, for as long as it takes, until you can't wait to stand up and give it.

That's the difference between a speech that's *fine* and one people still talk about months later. And it's why, year after year, my clients leave the reviews they do.

## My promise
- **100% original.** Every speech written from scratch, for one person.
- **In your voice.** It should sound like the best possible version of you — never like a writer.
- **Unlimited revisions.** We don't stop until it's right.
- **Honest, friendly, and discreet** from first message to final draft.
